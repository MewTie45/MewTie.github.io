    To make everyone's drive-in experience enjoyable, we ask that you abide by the following :
  1. All Trucks, Vans, SUV's and large vehicles must park next to the yellow speaker poles.
  2. Pets permitted provided they are on a leash and cleaned up after.
  3. Rainchecks are only issued for loss of power.
  4. Take only one parking space. Place your chairs, blankets, etc. in front of your vehicle.
  5. Keep rear hatches at a level so that they don't obstruct the view of those behind you. We will provide string to tie them down.
  6. Please use your parking lights while entering and exiting the drive-in.
  7. Please remember to keep your foot off the brake pedal. Brake lights disturb the people behind you.
  8. For children's safety, please don't let them run unattended especially as the movies let out and cars are exiting.
  9. Be considerate of those around you - don't be loud or disruptive.
  10. Please use the red trash cans located throughout the drive-in to discard your trash.
    Our employees have the right to enforce these rules. Any verbal abuse, threats or similar actions will result in removal from the drive-in without refund.
